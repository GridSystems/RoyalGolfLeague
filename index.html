General Rules

All scoring is nett (handicap adjusted).

Playing Handicap = Course Handicap × 95%, where Course Handicap = Handicap Index × (Slope ÷ 113) + (Course Rating − Par).

Handicap Index is as per the official DGA (Danish Golf Association) index. If found to be incorrect, it will be restated retroactively.

The season pot is split 50/50 between the Eclectic and Best 4 IPS competitions.

All payouts are made after MobilePay transaction fees.

Best 4 IPS — Individual Points ScoreSeason Competition

Stableford scoring across all rounds played during the season. Only your 4 best rounds count towards your season total.

How points are calculated per hole:

Points = max(0, 2 + Par + Handicap Strokes − Gross Score)
  • Score 2 over nett par → 0 pts
  • Score 1 over nett par (Bogey) → 1 pt
  • Score nett par → 2 pts
  • Score 1 under nett par (Birdie) → 3 pts
  • Score 2 under nett par (Eagle) → 4 pts

In the event of a tie, the players split the prize fund for the positions covered by the tied players. If two players are tied at the top, they split the prize fund for 1st and 2nd place equally, and so on.

Eclectic — Dream CardSeason Competition

Your personal best nett score on each individual hole, accumulated across all rounds in the season. Your dream card is built hole-by-hole — every time you play better than your previous best on a hole, it updates automatically.

  • Nett score = Gross score − Handicap strokes allocated to that hole
  • Lower nett score always replaces a previous best
  • If two rounds produce the same nett score, the one with more Stableford points is kept
  • You must have played a hole at least once for it to appear on your card

Season ranking is by lowest total nett score across all 18 holes. In the event of a tie, the players split the prize fund for the positions covered by the tied players. If two players are tied at the top, they split the prize fund for 1st and 2nd place equally, and so on.
